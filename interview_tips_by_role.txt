# Interview Tips by Role

## Backend Developer
- Focus on system design, scalability, and APIs.
- Practice explaining database schema and ORM concepts.
- Be prepared for questions about caching and performance tuning.

**Common Mistake:** Not testing edge cases in code challenges.

## Data Analyst
- Prepare to explain charts and graphs.
- Be ready to interpret data trends and anomalies.
- Know how to clean and validate datasets.

**Common Mistake:** Ignoring business context when analyzing data.

## Product Manager
- Demonstrate leadership and cross-functional communication.
- Practice feature prioritization and stakeholder negotiation.
- Know your favorite product and why it succeeds.

**Common Mistake:** Focusing only on tech, not user impact.
