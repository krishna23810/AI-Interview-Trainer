# Soft Skill Training Tips

## Communication
- Practice active listening.
- Use clear and concise language.
- Confirm understanding by summarizing key points.

## Teamwork
- Offer help when others are struggling.
- Acknowledge team contributions during discussions.
- Be open to feedback and diverse perspectives.

## Leadership
- Lead by example.
- Delegate tasks based on team membersâ€™ strengths.
- Maintain accountability and transparency.

## Adaptability
- Embrace change by staying calm under pressure.
- Prioritize tasks logically when faced with disruption.
- Show resilience by learning from setbacks.

## Problem Solving
- Clearly define the problem before jumping to solutions.
- Break problems down into smaller steps.
- Consider both short-term and long-term consequences.
